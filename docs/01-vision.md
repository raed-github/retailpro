# RetailPro Vision

## Purpose

RetailPro is a cloud-native retail management platform designed for small and medium businesses.

The system provides:

- Product Management
- Category Management
- Inventory Management
- POS System
- Sales Tracking
- Reporting
- Monitoring
- Kubernetes Deployment

## Users

### Admin

Full system access.

### Manager

Can manage products, inventory and reports.

### Cashier

Can perform sales operations.

## Technology Stack

Backend
